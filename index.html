Our Missions

We have delivered Wastage or Donations to needy Peoples. Our mission is to combat food waste by connecting surplus food with those in need. We strive to create a community where surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores,colleges canteen/mess and events finds its way to hungry mouths instead of ending up in landfills. Through our platform, we facilitate donations, provide resources for reducing food waste at home, and raise awareness about the environmental and social impacts of wasting food. By fostering partnerships and promoting sustainable practices, we aim to build a more equitable and environmentally conscious society where every meal counts and no food goes to waste.

What We Do & Why We Do

At Save Food Wastage Foundation, we're passionate about reducing food waste. Every day, we strive to connect surplus food with those who need it most. Our mission is clear: to combat hunger, protect the environment, and promote sustainability. We believe that every meal matters and every resource counts. By providing a platform for businesses and individuals to share excess food, we're not only saving perfectly good food from going to waste but also making a positive impact on communities and the planet.
